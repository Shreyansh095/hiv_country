Global HIV Situation in 2024

The fight against HIV/AIDS continues to be one of the biggest public health challenges of our time. Different countries experience varying rates of HIV prevalence due to healthcare access, education, socio-economic factors, and cultural practices.

Below is a table highlighting HIV prevalence rates in selected countries, along with population estimates. Understanding these numbers is crucial in directing resources where they are needed the most.

Conclusion

While significant progress has been made in reducing new HIV infections, much work remains. Strong public health strategies, inclusive healthcare systems, and international collaboration are the keys to overcoming this global health crisis.
